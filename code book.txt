Code Book

The variables in the tidy dataset are described as follows:

Activity: Descriptive label indicating the activity performed by the subject (e.g., walking, standing).
Subject: Identifier for the subject who performed the activity.
Other Variables: Each variable represents a specific measurement related to accelerometer 
and gyroscope readings during various activities. 
The variable names have been standardized according to the following conventions:

Acc: Accelerometer
Gyro: Gyroscope
Body: Body acceleration signals
Magnitude: Magnitude of the signals
Time: Time domain signals
Frequency: Frequency domain signals
Mean: Mean value of the measurements
STD: Standard deviation of the measurements
Angle: Angle measurements
Gravity: Gravity measurements
